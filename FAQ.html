Frequently asked questions

Here at Castle Guard, we know that the security industry can seem like a complicated place. We are here to answer your questions about no contract security monitoring and no credit check security systems.

Do you do credit checks?

No, we don't do credit checks for any of our residential security systems. If you have a large commercial job or multi-family install there may be additional steps to procure our service.

Do you have contracts?

For residential or commercial security monitoring, there is no contract and you can cancel at any time. No contract security monitoring is a month to month service unless you purchase a year at a time, which is how you get the best price.

How are your prices so affordable?

We have been in the industry for over 20 years and are a small family-owned shop located in Kansas City. This keeps our prices low.

What systems can you convert to Castle Guard monitoring?

We can monitor most systems including ADT, DSC, Honeywell and GE. If your system cannot be converted, we can replace your control panel for an additional one time fee. Call for details.

Do you install the systems or do I have install them?

Castle Guard installs systems or can sell you a do-it-yourself kit that is easily isntalled following the instructions we've created.
